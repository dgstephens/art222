Project One: Southworth Planetarium Logo

In this first project for ART222 I was asked to create a logo for the university's Southworth Planetarium. The tool of choice for this project was Adobe Illustrator. Learn more here.

Project Two: (x)topia duet

In (x)topia my intention was to explore the concept of loops as we perceive them in space and time through the flat, rectangular confines of a collage assembled from found images. Was I successful? Not entirely. But you can see for yourself here

Project Three: Under the Galaxy (tonight)

This found footage video project explores the theme of Yesterday's Tomorrow within the context of 1950s found footage of "kitchens of tomorrow" and the "future of space." It also explores what happens when these images are recontextualized through the inclusion of music, in this case, a remix of two songs which are both (loosely) thematically related to space.
